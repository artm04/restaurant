Philadephia sushi

Philadelphia Original rolls, prepared according to an authentic recipe, are a time-tested classic that is still one of the flagships of Japanese cuisine around the world. The traditional combination of salmon, rice, soft cream cheese and the freshness of cucumber with avocado make Philadelphia an extremely bright and appetizing dish, the variety of flavors of which allows you to abstract from everything mundane and move to the land of the Morning Sun.

Hunkan spice shrimp

Gunkan Spice Salmon is one of the representatives of traditional Japanese cuisine with a spicy taste. Spice sauce adds fire to the salmon and reveals its "buttery" essence as best as possible. Lovers of spice and simplicity will definitely like it.

Salmon sushi

Prepared according to a classic and time-tested recipe, sushi salmon is a wonderful combination of traditional Japanese minimalism, as well as a distinctive culinary aesthetic, thanks to which this dish best reveals the style, the power of the land where the sun rises.
